Baseball For Dummies

Baseball is an American team sport loosely related to Rounders and Cricket. Almost exclusively among major American sports, in baseball the defensive players handle the ball. Offensive players, once they successfully put the ball in play, must avoid it to advance and eventually score. Although first played in the US, baseball has become popular around the world with professional leagues throughout Latin America, the Carribean and Asia.

These are the ten positions in baseball:


Position Number Position Name Postion Abbreviation
1* Pitcher P
2 Catcher C
3 First Base 1b
4 Second Base 2b
5 Third Base 3b
6 Short Stop SS
7 Left Field LF
8 Center Field CF
9 Right Field RF
10** Designated Hitter DH
*Pitcher does not bat.
**Designated hitter does not play in the field.
