Major League Baseball

Major League Baseball's thirty teams are divided into two leagues - the National League and the American League. Each league is further divided into three five team divisions, roughly based on geographic location. At the end of the 162 game regular season, the best teams in each league play a tournament style playoff. The final winning team from each league play in a best of seven series called the World Series. The first team to win four of the seven games is delacred World Series Champion and receives the World Series Trophy.
